Summary: ToneFit collects minimal data needed to provide our guitar tone matching service. We never sell your personal information. You can delete your account and data at any time.

1 Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create a ToneFit account, we collect:

  • Email address and name (when signing up via email or Google)
  • Profile picture (if signing in via Google)
  • Password (stored as a secure hash — never plain text)

Gear & Usage Data

  • Your saved guitar, amp, pedal, and effects unit selections
  • Songs you've researched and saved tone settings
  • Your gear presets created during onboarding
  • Subscription plan and payment status

Automatically Collected Data

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited and features used within ToneFit

2 How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the ToneFit service
  • Personalize tone recommendations based on your gear setup
  • Process your subscription and manage billing
  • Send important account notifications and service updates
  • Respond to your support requests and feedback
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

We do not use your data for targeted advertising or sell it to third parties.

3 Data Storage & Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — never stored in plain text
  • Database access is restricted to authorized systems only
  • We use Supabase (PostgreSQL) hosted on AWS for data storage

4 Cookies & Tracking

ToneFit uses essential cookies to keep you logged in, remember your gear preferences, and maintain your subscription status. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

5 Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth — for sign-in
  • Anthropic Claude API — powers tone research
  • Razorpay / Stripe — payment processing
  • Vercel — website hosting
  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • iTunes Search API — song search autocomplete

6 Your Rights

  • Access — Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion — Delete your account and all associated data permanently
  • Export — Download your saved tones and gear data
  • Opt-out — Unsubscribe from non-essential emails at any time

Email us at contact@tonefit.com to exercise any of these rights.

7 Children's Privacy

ToneFit is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Contact us at contact@tonefit.com if you believe a child has provided us with data.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the date at the top and notify registered users by email for significant changes.

9 Contact Us

  • Website: tonefit.site
  • Response time: Within 7 business days
