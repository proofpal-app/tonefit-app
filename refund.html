Refund Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026 Version 1.0
Our Promise: We want you to love ToneFit. If you're not satisfied within 48 hours of your first payment, we'll give you a full refund — no questions asked.

1 48-Hour Money-Back Guarantee

If you subscribe to any ToneFit paid plan and are not satisfied, you may request a full refund within 48 hours of your first payment. This applies to:

  • First-time subscribers on any plan (Beginner or Expert)
  • Monthly and annual subscriptions
  • Upgrades from one plan to another

To request a refund within 48 hours, email us at contact@tonefit.com with your account email and we will process it within 1 business day.

2 Free Trial

All ToneFit plans include a 7-day free trial. You will not be charged during the trial period. Your subscription only begins after the trial ends.

  • No credit card required to start the free trial
  • Cancel anytime during the trial with zero charges
  • If you forget to cancel, you may still request a refund within 48 hours of the first charge

3 Refund Eligibility Summary

Situation Refund Eligible?
Within 48 hours of first payment ✓ Yes — Full Refund
Cancel during free trial ✓ Yes — No Charge
After 48 hours of first payment ✗ No
Renewal payments (2nd month+) ✗ No
Partial month after cancellation ✗ No
Account suspended for Terms violation ✗ No

4 How to Cancel Your Subscription

You can cancel your ToneFit subscription at any time:

  • Log in to your ToneFit account
  • Go to Account → Subscription
  • Click Cancel Subscription
  • Your access continues until the end of your current billing period

Alternatively, email us at contact@tonefit.com and we'll cancel it for you within 1 business day.

5 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund within the eligible 48-hour window:

  • Email contact@tonefit.com
  • Subject line: Refund Request — [your email]
  • Include your account email and reason (optional)
  • We will process within 1 business day
  • Refund appears on your card within 5-10 business days

6 Exceptions

We reserve the right to deny refunds in cases of:

  • Suspected fraud or abuse of the refund policy
  • Accounts suspended for violating our Terms of Use
  • Requests made after the 48-hour window

7 Contact Us

For any refund questions or requests:

  • Email: contact@tonefit.com
  • Response time: Within 1 business day
